  • I assigned each survivor its specific new variable.
    The new variable described how many points from the initial religious value the survivor's religious belief decreased after the war.
  • There will be two scenarios in my final paper. Each of them will be based on "True" and "Wrong" data sets.
  • The main distinction I am going to make is that, by dropping a portion of the survivors whose interviews did not give any information about their current religion, we can conclude that the majority of survivors lost their faith after the war.
  • Contrary, by keeping those survivors who did not explicitly mention anything about their religion,
    we can also conclude that the change in religious beliefs after the war was not as statistically significant as it might seem.
